Nepali Favourite

Momo

Soft dumplings filled with vegetables or meat, served with spicy achar. Momo is one of Nepal’s most iconic and widely loved foods.

Traditional Meal

Dal Bhat Tarkari

Nepal’s classic daily meal made with rice, lentil soup, vegetable curry, and pickles. It is nutritious, balanced, and strongly connected to Nepali food identity.

⏱ 50 mins 🔥 Easy

Festival Dish
